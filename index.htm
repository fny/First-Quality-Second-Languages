Who We Are

First Quality Second Languages, a Florida language school established in 1983, offers expert instruction and translation services in more than 30 foreign languages, as well as English as a Second Language.
Covering a large portion of Florida, FQSL has teachers, tutors and translators available in 10 counties including Broward, Palm Beach, Martin, Orange and Seminole. Through FQSL, clients find multi-service features, a large spectrum of languages, a professional staff of linguists and an abundant choice of educational settings.

Details of our Services

  • On site corporate group courses tailored to your business needs
  • Small group classes to enhance your business or personal goals
  • Private instruction at your pace with your choice of curriculum
  • Tutoring to pinpoint and erase the problem areas that cannot be addressed in a classroom setting.
  • Classes for children 6 and older to stimulate their minds and to set a positive tone for future language learning
  • Translation services from and into more than 30 languages done professionally and promptly
  • Interpretations enabling clients to transact business and/or legal matters
